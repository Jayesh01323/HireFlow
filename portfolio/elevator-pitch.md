## Pitch Tips

### General Tips

1. **Start with the Problem**: Hook your audience with the problem you're solving
2. **Show Impact**: Use numbers and metrics to demonstrate impact
3. **Be Authentic**: Share your personal connection to the problem
4. **Keep it Simple**: Avoid jargon, speak clearly
5. **End with Call to Action**: What do you want the listener to do?

### Tailoring Tips

1. **Know Your Audience**: Adjust technical depth based on audience
2. **Focus on Value**: Highlight what matters to the specific audience
3. **Use Their Language**: Use terminology familiar to the audience
4. **Address Their Needs**: Solve problems relevant to the audience
5. **Provide Evidence**: Back up claims with data and examples

### Delivery Tips

1. **Practice**: Rehearse your pitch until it flows naturally
2. **Be Enthusiastic**: Show passion for your project
3. **Make Eye Contact**: Connect with your audience
4. **Use Gestures**: Emphasize key points with body language
5. **Be Prepared for Questions**: Anticipate and prepare for follow-up questions

## Common Questions & Answers

### Q: What makes HireFlow AI different from LinkedIn or Indeed?

**A:** LinkedIn and Indeed are job boards. HireFlow AI is an intelligence platform that analyzes your resume, matches you to jobs based on skills, provides career coaching, and gives you analytics to improve your job search strategy. We don't just show jobs - we help you understand which jobs you're qualified for and how to improve your chances.

### Q: How accurate is your AI parsing?

**A:** We achieve 95% accuracy in resume parsing using Google Gemini API. This includes extracting skills, experience, education, and projects. We've tested on hundreds of resumes and continuously improve based on user feedback.

### Q: Is this only for software engineers?

**A:** Currently, we focus on software engineering roles because that's our expertise. However, our architecture is designed to be extensible, and we plan to expand to other fields like data science, product management, and design in future versions.

### Q: How do you make money?

**A:** We're exploring a freemium model. Basic features like resume parsing and job matching will be free. Premium features like personalized career coaching, company insights, salary estimation, and priority matching will be paid subscriptions.

### Q: What's your competitive advantage?

**A:** Our AI-first approach, focus on the underserved fresher segment, comprehensive feature set, and open-source community. We're not just a job board - we're an intelligence platform that helps users make data-driven career decisions.
